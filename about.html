WEI TU

Urban Researcher/Data analyst

        Wei Tu is senior associate research fellow at the department of geo-spatial information engineering, School of Architecture and Urban Planning, Shenzhen University. He is a visiting scholar at Senseable City Lab, MIT. He obtained the Ph.D from State Key Laboratory of Survey, Mapping and Remote Sensing, Wuhan University.

        His research interests focus on the urban informatics, including automatics recognition of human activity-mobility from multi-source urban data, trajectory (i.e., taxi GPS, bus GPS, smart card data, social media, data, mobile phone data, and etc.) modelling, analysis and optimization, new analysis tool in dynamic-GIS. Wei Tu is always looking for motivated interns/students interested in GIS, big data, and urban studies. Please contact me for details!
